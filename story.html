    This phase introduces some of the main characters: Iron Man, Captain America, Thor and Hulk, plus of the organizations S.H.I.E.L.D. (Extra-governmental military intelligence and anti-terrorism agency of the United States, responsible for maintaining global security, who is responsible for the formation of the Avengers) and HYDRA (Theorist- criminal - paramilitary organization dedicated to the world domination) and the context from which the universe will develop. In Captain America appears the first Gem of Infinity, the Third Act (Gem of Space). Later the villians Loki and Thanos ally to try to steal the Third Act and conquer the Earth, they possess the Gem of the Mind. The Avengers defeat Loky at the Battle of New York.
