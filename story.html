MCU's plot (Marvel Cinematic Universe)

The origin of the universe: There was a powerful explosion many years ago which created the universe. This originated the 6 Infinity Stones, each one controls an aspect of existence. These are the following: 


        1. Space Stone
        2. Mind Stone
        3. Reality Stone
        4. Power Stone
        5. Time Stone
        6. Soul Stone

    This phase introduces some of the main characters like Iron Man, Captain America, Thor and Hulk. Plus the organizations S.H.I.E.L.D. and HYDRA. The first one, an extra-governmental military intelligence and anti-terrorism agency of the United States, responsible for maintaining global security, which is responsible for the formation of the Avengers. HYDRA, a theorist, criminal, paramilitary organization whose main objective is world domination.

    The first Infinity Stone appears in Captain America, as the Tesseract (Space Stone). Later the villans Loki and Thanos ally to try to steal the Tesseract and conquer the Earth, previous they possess the Mind Stone. The Avengers defeat Loki at the Battle of New York.
